Who are we?

In 2012, from the sun and sand of Goa, a team of automotive enthusiasts set up on a mission to build an open wheel, open cockpit formula type race car. After a couple of years of research and effort, the team was realized in 2014 and was called "Team Grease Monkeys", which has now become the official Formula Student team of Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani - K. K. Birla Goa Campus.

The team began its journey by participating in the SAEINDIA Supra event held at Formula racing circuit in Coimbatore under the name ' Grease Monkeys' with the car SIEG 1.0. Since then the team has been participating at Buddh International Circuit in Greater Noida over the last 3 years. Thus started the SIEG legacy. Over the years the team has been the sole participant from the state of Goa. The most recent car SIEG 3.0 participated in the SAE Supra 2017 event and received accolades from the judges. With a 50+ strong team, immense knowledge and sheer dedication towards motorsports, the team is ready to take on upcoming challenges in the 2018-19 season.
